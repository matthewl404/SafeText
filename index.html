What is Phishing?

Phishing is when someone tries to trick you into giving them your information like your password.

How to Spot Phishing:

  • Be careful with messages that say things like “Urgent!”, or “You’ve won!”
  • Check the link before clicking. Does it look strange or fake?
  • Look at the sender's email. Does it match the real company?
  • Don’t share passwords or personal info over text or email.

Stay Safe:

  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Ask an adult or friend if you're unsure
